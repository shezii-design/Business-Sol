🔒 Privacy Policy

Hisab Desk — Last updated: March 2026

Your business data belongs to you and only you. We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party or external organization.

What is Hisab Desk?

Hisab Desk is a simple business management system designed for small and medium-sized businesses. It helps you manage products, sales, purchases, customers, suppliers, inventory, and financial records — all in one secure platform.

What Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect and store:

  • Account Information: Your email address and full name to identify your account
  • Business Information: Business name, phone number, and address (optional)
  • Business Data: All data you enter including products, sales, purchases, customers, suppliers, expenses, and invoices

All business data you enter is stored securely in your private account and is only accessible by you.

How Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored in a secure cloud database (Supabase) with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled. This means:

  • Each user can only access their own data
  • It is technically impossible for any other user to access your data
  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Regular automated backups are performed

Who Can See Your Data

Only you. We do not share your business data with any third party. Your data is protected by:

  • Database-level security (Row Level Security)
  • User authentication and authorization
  • Encrypted connections (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing

FBR & Tax Authorities

Hisab Desk is a private business management tool. It is not connected to FBR, any POS integration system, or any government reporting infrastructure. We do not automatically report any of your business transactions to any authority.

If a government authority contacts us with a valid court order regarding a specific account, we are legally required to comply. However, we will always notify the account holder where legally permitted to do so.

Your Right to Delete Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Specific Records: You can delete individual products, sales, customers, etc. at any time from within the app
  • Delete All Data: From Settings → Danger Zone → Delete All My Data, you can permanently remove all your business records from our database. This action is immediate, permanent, and cannot be undone.
  • Delete Account: To permanently delete your entire account, contact us via WhatsApp at 0326-6450963

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS encrypted connections
  • Your password is never stored in plain text
  • We use Supabase's enterprise-grade security infrastructure, trusted by thousands of companies worldwide
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security audits are performed

Cookies & Tracking

We respect your privacy:

  • No advertising cookies — We don't track you for marketing
  • No behavior tracking — We don't sell your data
  • No advertisements — Ever
  • Essential cookies only — Your login session is stored locally so you don't have to sign in every time

Subscription & Payment Information

If you subscribe to Hisab Desk:

  • We collect your payment confirmation (screenshot) sent via WhatsApp
  • We do not store credit card or banking information
  • Payments are processed through JazzCash (offline)
  • Your subscription status (active/expired) is stored in your profile

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy in a way that affects your data rights, we will:

  • Notify you via a banner inside the app before the change takes effect
  • Give you at least 30 days notice
  • Allow you to export all your data before any changes

Your Rights

Under privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (you can view/export it anytime in the app)
  • Correct your data (edit any record in the app)
  • Delete your data (delete all data from Settings)
  • Export your data (coming soon: PDF/Excel export)
  • Object to processing (contact us to close your account)
